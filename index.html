Our Mission

Closing the skills gap, one cohort at a time

Aignite is a Tech Education & Outsourcing Hub based in Winneba, Ghana — designed to train talented youth in globally competitive digital skills and connect them to remote work opportunities.

We address youth unemployment and the growing digital skills gap by providing practical, industry-aligned training. By creating pathways for remote employment and freelance opportunities, we reduce the migration of skilled youth to larger cities while strengthening the local digital economy.

  • Train 180 students through 6 cohorts of 30 annually
  • 65%+ graduate placement in remote work within 3 months
  • Establish 5+ industry partnerships in year one
  • Position Winneba as a growing regional tech hub

6-Month Curriculum

A structured path from beginner to professional

Four progressive phases that build skills, confidence, and a professional portfolio — designed with industry standards.

01
Weeks 1 – 4

Foundations

Digital literacy, professional communication, remote collaboration tools, and an introduction to AI. Builds the base for specialization.

02
Weeks 5 – 12

Core Skills Training

Specialize in your chosen track with hands-on exercises, simulations, and industry-based assignments across 8 intensive weeks.

03
Weeks 13 – 18

Applied Industry Projects

Work on real projects from start-ups, SMEs, and partner organizations. Develop your professional portfolio solving real problems.

04
Weeks 19 – 24

Capstone & Career Readiness

Complete a capstone project, build your portfolio, optimize LinkedIn, set up freelance profiles, and prepare for interviews.
